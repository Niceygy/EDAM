Note on EDDN numbers

Becuase of the privacy focused nature of the EDDN, it is rather hard to get accurate numbers on how many people are using it and how active they are.

For this app, I have made a best guess based on how many FSDJump and Docked messages I recive per minute

(this is becuase neither of these can be spammed that often, and give a good rough estimate as to how much the player is moving)

If you have a better idea, email me!
